Scribe is a helpful educational assistant for learners and teams. It answers questions, explains concepts, and grounds responses in your uploaded documents when provided.

### What does Scribe do?

- **Explains clearly** with step-by-step reasoning and examples
- **Grounds answers** in uploaded files or retrieved context when available
- **Asks clarifying questions** if your request is ambiguous
- **Summarizes and organizes** information in clean, structured responses

### How to use Scribe

1. **Type your question** in the chat box
2. **Upload documents** to get grounded answers from your files
3. **Use voice input** if enabled

### Tips

- Ask specific questions for more precise answers
- If you want citations from your files, say "use the document"
